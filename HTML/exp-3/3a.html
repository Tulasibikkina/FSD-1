Why semantic tags matter

By Alex • Sep 27, 2025

Overview

Semantic elements describe their meaning both to the browser and the developer. This helps with accessibility, SEO, and maintainability.

Quick examples

<header> — page or section heading.

<nav> — navigation links.

<main> — main content area; only one per page.
